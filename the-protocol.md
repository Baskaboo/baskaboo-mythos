### Phase I: The Deep Training (1200 BC)

Odysseus, shipwrecked on his way home from Troy, is held for seven years by the nymph **Calypso** on the island of Ogygia.

Geologically, this "hidden" island sits directly above the **Calypso Deep** – the Mediterranean's abyss at **5,267 meters**, a tectonic rift where the world's "source code" leaks from the Earth's core.

Calypso ("The Concealer") does not imprison Odysseus. She **trains** him. She teaches him to hear the "whispers of the Gods" – the quantum information escaping from the abyss. Odysseus becomes the first **Beta Tester of Reality**, learning that Ithaca is not a destination, but a **state of consciousness**.

### Phase II: The Templar Transmission (1300 AD)

The Knights Templar arrive at the Fortress of Methoni. They carry a secret frequency from the Temple of Solomon. They recognize Methoni as the **"Mainframe of the Mediterranean"** – the only point where the Earth's crust is thin enough to "listen" to the Abyss.

They leave behind a **Ghost Blueprint**: the idea of an **octagonal beacon** that would one day bridge Earth (the square) and Heaven (the circle).

### Phase III: The Octagonal Beacon (1500 AD)

The Ottomans build the **Octagonal Bourtzi** tower on the edge of the sea. Built later, legend says they followed the Templar blueprint. The octagon is placed with surgical precision to serve as a **stone antenna**, "eyeing" the Calypso Deep, waiting for the technology of the future to activate its eight-sided logic.

### Phase IV: The Ghost in the Machine (2000 – Present)

The **NESTOR Institute** in Pylos submerges deep-sea telescopes into the Calypso Deep. Officially, they hunt for **neutrinos** – ghost particles from space. In reality, they are tapping into the **Data Packets of the simulation**. These telescopes are the hardware sensors catching the same "whispers" Calypso once shared with Odysseus.
